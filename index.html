AkiraChix Campus

My First week's experience

From the moment I arrived on campus, the onboarding experience was so seamless.The friendly staff greeted me warmly and provided all the necessary materials i required. They conducted an orientation session that helped us familiarize more with the campus, their vision and also the program. AkiraChix trainers are exceptional. The first introduction sessions allowed us to learn, interact and actively engage with the them. Their approach was hands-on and interactive making the lectures more enjoyable. AkiraChix trainers are approachable hence making it easy for students to answer and ask questions in class.


AkiraChix campus itself was a sight to behold.The environment was very clean with well maintained study areas and garden. Our class setups were supplied with comfortable seats, screens and personal laptops. Hospitality from the alumnis was very heartwarming. They offered great advice and shared their personal experiences with us. This was a good way to encourage us even as we kickstart our journey.They also assited us and made sure that we settled in our respective classes and room with ease.

Good things I experienced:

  1. Heartwarming hospitality by alumni and staff.
  2. Clear orientation session conducted on the first day.
  3. Well outlined AkiraChix safeguarding policy and handbook.

Bad things I experienced:

  • Cold weather conditions of the area 🥶
  • Culture shocks from diffrent communities represented in the campus 😳

In conclusion, first week in AkiraChix was totally amazing..!
